Kureci rizek s bramborovou kasi

Description

A recipe for the traditional Czech meal consisting of a (deep) fried chicken breast.

Typically served with mashed potatoes or potato salad.

Ingredients (Rizek)

  • 500 g of chicken (breasts, alternatively thighs)
  • Salt
  • Ground pepper
  • Finely ground flour (US/Canada equivalent all-purpose flour, UK plain flour)
  • Breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 ml of milk
  • Frying oil

Steps - rizek

  1. Cut the meat into slices and tenderize them lightly.
  2. Salt and pepper the slices from both sides.
  3. Prepare 3 soup plates/bowls
    • Pour the flour in the first one.
    • Crack the two eggs into the second one, add a dash of salt and pepper, milk, and properly mix everything.
    • Put the breadcrumbs in the last one.
  4. Coat the meat slices thoroughly in the first plate/bowl, then in the second one and lastly in the third one.
  5. Pour sufficient amount of oil on a frying pan and fry the coated slices until golden brown.
  6. Serve with mashed potatoes or potato salad.
